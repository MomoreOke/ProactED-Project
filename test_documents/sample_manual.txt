EQUIPMENT MAINTENANCE MANUAL
Model: XYZ-500 Industrial Pump

ROUTINE MAINTENANCE SCHEDULE:
- Weekly inspection of seals and gaskets
- Monthly lubrication of bearings
- Quarterly replacement of filters
- Semi-annual alignment check
- Annual complete overhaul

PREVENTIVE MAINTENANCE:
1. Check oil levels daily
2. Monitor vibration levels weekly
3. Inspect for leaks monthly
4. Replace worn parts as needed
5. Schedule professional inspection every 6 months

TROUBLESHOOTING:
- Unusual noise: Check alignment and lubrication
- Reduced flow: Clean or replace filters
- Overheating: Check coolant levels and ventilation

SAFETY NOTES:
- Always disconnect power before maintenance
- Use proper personal protective equipment
- Follow lockout/tagout procedures
